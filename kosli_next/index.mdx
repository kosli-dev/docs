Kosli Next is where we share what we're working on next, so customers can shape it before it ships.

It contains two kinds of content:

- **Concepts** - ideas and directions we're considering. Nothing here is built yet. We publish them to gather feedback on the problem and the proposed approach.
- **Preview** - real features available to opt-in customers ahead of general availability. Behaviour can still change.

Nothing in Kosli Next is part of Kosli's current product surface. To learn what Kosli does today, switch to the **Product** view.

## How to give feedback

Email [support@kosli.com](mailto:support@kosli.com) with the page name and your thoughts. We read every message.
